PoolRadar

Track your favorite pool players, venues, and tournament directors across DigitalPool tournaments.

Getting started: Paste a DigitalPool tournament URL in the top bar. Click any player name to add them as a favorite. Click the venue or TD buttons to save those too.

Favorites feed: Once you have favorites, the tournament feed auto-populates with recent tournaments (+/- 3 days) involving your favorites.

Duplicate accounts: PoolRadar automatically detects and groups duplicate DigitalPool accounts by matching Fargo ID and name slug variations.

Live updates: Match detail auto-refreshes every 60 seconds for in-progress tournaments.

All data is stored in your browser. No server, no account needed.
